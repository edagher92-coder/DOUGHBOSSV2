Important note on the Privacy Act threshold. Many small businesses with an annual turnover of $3 million or less are exempt from the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) under the "small business operator" exemption (s 6D). However, exceptions apply — including businesses that disclose personal information for a benefit, service or advantage, and businesses related to a larger entity. Dough Boss should confirm with its adviser whether it is an "APP entity" bound by the Act. This policy is written to apply the Australian Privacy Principles as best practice regardless of the technical threshold, which is the recommended approach for a customer-facing food business handling email, payment and marketing data.

Plain-English summary (please read this box)

  • We are Dough Boss (and our dessert brand Snow Boss), a Mediterranean/Lebanese bakery with shops at Bankstown, Roselands and Revesby in NSW.
  • We collect personal information when you order online, request a catering quote, or sign up for the Snow Boss student voucher.
  • We use it to take and fulfil your order, give you a quote, process your payment, and (only if you opt in) send you marketing.
  • We share it with trusted service providers who help us run the business — including Stripe (payments), Formspree (the voucher sign-up form), our POSPal point-of-sale, and our email and website hosting providers. Some of these are overseas (mostly the United States and Ireland/EU).
  • You can opt out of marketing at any time, and you can ask to see or correct the information we hold about you.
  • If you have a concern, contact us first; you can also complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

2. What this policy covers

This policy applies to personal information we collect through:

  • our online ordering website (menu, custom pizza builder, cart and checkout);
  • our marketing website, which currently includes three forms that collect personal information — the catering quote form, the Snow Boss student voucher form, and (where offered) the online ordering checkout; and

It does not cover third-party websites we link to (see clause 13), which have their own privacy policies.

3. What personal information we collect

Personal information means information or an opinion about an identified individual, or an individual who is reasonably identifiable. We aim to collect only the information we reasonably need for the purposes described in this policy.

Depending on how you interact with us, we may collect:

3.1 Online ordering (checkout)

  • your name and phone number;
  • your order details (items, sizes, toppings, quantities, special instructions and notes);
  • for delivery orders, your delivery address and any access notes;
  • your pickup shop / location preference; and
  • if you pay online, payment information processed by our payment provider (see clause 3.4 and clause 5).

3.2 Catering quote form

  • your name, email address and phone number; and
  • the details of your catering enquiry (event, date, numbers, menu preferences and any message you include).

Submitting this form sends your enquiry by email to [orders@doughboss.com.au ___] so we can prepare a quote and contact you.

3.3 Snow Boss student voucher form

  • your student email address; and
  • any other information you choose to provide on the form (for example, to verify student status, if required).

The voucher form submits to a third-party form processor, Formspree (United States) — see clause 6.2 and clause 7.

When you pay online, your card details are entered into and processed by Stripe, our payment provider. We do not see or store your full card number. We may receive limited payment confirmation data from Stripe (for example, a payment reference, the last four digits of the card, the amount, the currency and the payment status) so we can confirm and reconcile your order.

4. How we collect personal information, and notice at the point of collection (APP 5)

We collect personal information directly from you when you place an order, complete one of our website forms, contact us, or interact with us in store or by phone.

At or before the point of collection (for example, beside the submit button on each form), we provide a short collection notice telling you who is collecting the information, why, who we may share it with, and how to find this policy. Ready-to-use microcopy for those notices is included at the end of this document.

Where it is reasonable and practicable, we collect personal information directly from you. If we collect information about you from a third party (for example, a delivery partner), we will take reasonable steps to ensure you are made aware of the matters in APP 5.

5. Why we collect, hold, use and disclose your information (APP 3 and APP 6)

We collect and use personal information for the following primary purposes:

  • to take, process, fulfil and deliver your orders (including pickup and delivery);
  • to prepare and provide catering quotes and manage catering bookings, deposits and balances;
  • to process payments and reconcile transactions (via Stripe and our POS, POSPal);
  • to administer the Snow Boss student voucher and verify eligibility for the launch offer;
  • to respond to your enquiries, requests and complaints;
  • to keep records required for tax, accounting, food-safety and business purposes; and
  • to protect the security of our systems and prevent fraud.

We may also use your information for related secondary purposes that you would reasonably expect, such as improving our menu and service. We will only use or disclose your information for a secondary purpose where the law allows (for example, where it is related to the primary purpose and within your reasonable expectations, where you have consented, or where it is otherwise permitted or required by law).

We use personal information for direct marketing only as described in clause 8.

8. Direct marketing and the Spam Act 2003 (Cth)

8.1 When we send marketing

We will send you marketing or promotional messages (such as offers, new menu items, and the Snow Boss launch offer) only where we are permitted to do so, consistent with the Australian Privacy Principles and the Spam Act 2003 (Cth).

8.2 The Snow Boss voucher launch list (consent)

The Snow Boss student voucher is a marketing sign-up. When you submit the voucher form to unlock the launch offer, you are consenting to join our Snow Boss marketing list and to receive launch and promotional messages by email (and, if you provide it and consent, by other channels). The form will make this clear at the point of collection (see the microcopy at the end of this policy). Under the Spam Act, we rely on your consent, we will identify ourselves as the sender, and we will include a functional unsubscribe in every commercial electronic message.

8.3 How to opt out / unsubscribe

You can opt out of marketing at any time by:

  • clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email; or

We will action unsubscribe requests promptly (and in any event within the time required by the Spam Act). Opting out of marketing does not stop us sending you transactional messages necessary to complete an order or quote you have asked for.
