Good to know

Catering FAQ

How much notice do you need?

48 hours for most orders, and 72 hours for 40+ guests.

Do you cater dietary requirements?

Yes — everything is halal, with vegetarian and vegan options on every package.

What's the minimum order?

$150. The Lunch Run ($165) feeds 8–10 and is our most popular weekday option.

Pickup or delivery?

Both. Delivery is free within 8km of a shop, $25 up to 15km, quoted beyond. Pickup is always free.

How does the deposit work?

A 30% deposit secures your date. The balance is due 48 hours before the event.
