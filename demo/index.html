Who we are

A taste of the Mediterranean,
since 2009.

Dough Boss is a contemporary Lebanese bakery preserving traditional dough recipes passed down through generations, while embracing a modern twist. We've been in the industry since 2009, using only the finest natural ingredients to craft simple, delicious manoush, pizza and pies — a taste of the Mediterranean in every bite.
