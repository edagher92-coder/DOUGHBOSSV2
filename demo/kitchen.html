What am I looking at?

The staff kitchen tablet: orders flow left to right — New → Preparing → Ready — and every button works on the simulated orders.

  • Fresh card · amber after 5 min in the kitchen · red after 10 min — time is accelerated here (1 s = 30 s) so you can watch it happen. The timer pill on each card ages with it.
  • PAID paid online by card · COLLECT $X take payment at handover (bolder on Ready cards) · LATER scheduled order sitting calm until it's due, with its ⏰ pickup window.
  • Heartbeat (top right): Live real-time connection · Polling fallback refresh · Offline connection lost — try “Simulate connection drop”.
  • Accept a New order with a ready-time (10/15/20/30 min) — the customer's phone beside the board shows the email + live-tracker update they'd receive. Every change offers a 10-second UNDO; completed/cancelled orders come back from Recent.
  • The All day strip totals items across active orders so the kitchen can batch bakes; the Today tiles above show orders, gross, paid-by-card and still-to-collect at a glance.
