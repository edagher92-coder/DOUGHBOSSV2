# Dough Boss — Own Your Orders
### A Business Case for Building Dough Boss's Own Online Ordering System

**Prepared for:** The Owner, Dough Boss
**Date:** 17 June 2026
**Re:** Funding and launching Dough Boss's own commission-free online ordering platform

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Dough Boss has a rare opportunity sitting right under its roof. Your website already has a working online ordering engine installed and healthy — it has simply never been switched on. We can change that. This project turns your existing site into your own branded ordering system where **customer orders appear instantly on a screen in your kitchen**, staff act on them the moment they land, customers pay by card, and you keep **100% of every dollar and 100% of your customer data**. Unlike Uber Eats, Menulog, Slice and QR-code apps that take roughly **7–35% of every order** and own your customers, this system charges **zero per-order commission**. The first phase — the live kitchen order board — is already in development and can be ready in about **1–2 weeks**.

## 3. The Vision — Own Your Orders

Every order placed through Uber Eats or Menulog comes with two hidden costs: the **commission** they skim, and the **customer** they keep. You never learn who ordered, so you can never bring them back yourself — you have to rent them again next time.

This project flips that:

- **Your site. Your orders. Your customers.**
- **No middleman taking a cut** of every pizza.
- A **repeat-customer database you own**, which later powers cheaper, sharper advertising — because marketing to people who already love you costs far less than buying strangers.
- A **professional, branded experience** from "order now" to "ready for pickup."

---

## 4. How It Works (Simple Steps)

1. **Customer orders on your website** — they pick their pizzas, sizes and toppings on a clean, branded page.
2. **The order lands instantly on the kitchen screen** — a live board lights up with an **audible alert** the second it arrives. *This is the heart of the system.*
3. **Staff tap once to manage it** — Accept → Preparing → Ready. No paper lost, no order missed.
4. **The customer sees live status** — they know it's accepted and when it's ready, without phoning the shop.
5. **(From Stage 2) The customer pays by card** — money is settled automatically, with a proper GST tax invoice.

### Illustrative commission-savings example *(assumptions only — conservative, not a guarantee)*

> **Assumptions:** 150 orders/week, $35 average order value, 25% effective third-party commission avoided.
>
> - Weekly order value: 150 × $35 = **$5,250**
> - Commission avoided at 25%: **≈ $1,313/week**
> - **≈ $5,250–$5,700 kept per month** (≈ 4.33 weeks)

Even at half this volume, the kept margin comfortably exceeds the running cost of the whole system. *Your real numbers may differ — these are planning figures to show the shape of the opportunity, not a promise.*
