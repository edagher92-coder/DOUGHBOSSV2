Overview

What the system is

One system, three experiences

Dough Boss runs as a single ordering platform on your own website. Customers order and pay online, the kitchen works orders on an iPad, and you see everything across all three shops — no delivery-app middleman, no commission, your data.

1

Customers order

Browse the menu, build a pizza or pick a catering pack, choose pickup or delivery, and pay by card — all in the phone’s browser, no app to download.

2

The kitchen runs it

Each order lands on the right shop’s iPad board with an alert. Staff accept with one tap, set an ETA, and move it New → Preparing → Ready.

3

You stay in control

Payments settle into your Stripe account; sales, top items and customers show on one dashboard; you edit the menu and hours yourself.

How it works

End to end

From their phone to your kitchen

1

Order

Customer browses the menu, builds their pizza or picks a catering pack, chooses pickup or delivery.

→
2

Pay

Secure Stripe checkout. Funds settle into your Stripe account with an automatic GST receipt.

→
3

Route

→
4

Cook

→
5

Picked up or delivered. The order and the customer’s details are saved in your system.

Automatic shop routing

Delivery postcodes and pickup choice send each order to the right kitchen — no manual sorting between Bankstown, Revesby and Roselands.

Server-checked pricing

Every total is recalculated on the server in AUD with GST, so what the customer pays is always correct — never trusted from the browser.

Nothing to install

Customers order in their browser and staff use a normal iPad in Safari. No apps to install, no third-party tablets.

Rollout

How we go live

1

Set up · Week 1

Load your menu, branding and shop details; connect Stripe and WordPress; set up the kitchen iPads.

2

Test · Week 2

Run real orders through ordering, checkout, catering and the kitchen board with your team in test mode.

3

Go live

Switch to live payments across all three shops; we stay close through the first busy weekends.

WHAT WE NEED FROM YOU

  • Menu — items, sizes, prices
  • Shop details & hours (×3)
  • Your ABN (GST invoices)
  • WordPress access
  • Stripe account (we’ll help)
  • Logo & brand assets (high-res)
THE COMMERCIALS, IN SHORT
Setup ~$4–5k at cost · $800/week + 5% of online sales (capped 15%) to run, maintain & support it. Stripe’s ~1.7% + 30c per card is separate — a flat, capped fee with no per-order commission.
