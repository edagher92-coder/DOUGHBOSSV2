1. Executive summary

Dough Boss's digital system isn't yet strong enough to drive modern ordering, repeat customers, paid ads, local SEO, catering leads, or future multi-shop/franchise scale. This proposal transforms the website into DoughBoss Digital OS — a direct ordering, shop-operations and marketing platform, built in stages, inside the existing site, without disrupting the live business. We prove real online ordering and a live kitchen screen first, then expand into payments, marketing, multi-shop reporting and growth.

4. How it works

  1. A customer picks a shop, browses the menu, and builds their pizza on your website.
  2. They check out — paying by card online, or pay-on-pickup.
  3. That shop's kitchen screen lights up with an alarm the instant the order lands.
  4. Staff tap once to Accept (and give a time), then Preparing, then Ready.
  5. The customer watches live status on their phone.
