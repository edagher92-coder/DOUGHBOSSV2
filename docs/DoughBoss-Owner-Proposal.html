4How it works

  1. Customer picks a shop, builds their pizza, and checks out (card online or pay-on-pickup).
  2. That shop's kitchen screen lights up with an alarm the instant the order lands.
  3. Staff tap once: Accept (+ time) → Preparing → Ready.
  4. The customer watches live status on their phone.
