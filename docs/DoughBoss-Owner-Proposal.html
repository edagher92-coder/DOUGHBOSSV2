1. Executive summary

Dough Boss's digital system isn't yet strong enough to drive modern ordering, repeat customers, paid ads, local SEO, catering leads, or future multi-shop/franchise scale. This proposal transforms the website into DoughBoss Digital OS — not just a visual refresh, but a direct ordering, shop-operations and marketing platform. We build it in stages, inside the existing site, without disrupting the live business, proving real online ordering and a live kitchen screen first, then expanding into payments, marketing, multi-shop reporting and growth.

4. How it works

  1. A customer picks a shop, browses the menu, and builds their pizza on your website.
  2. They check out — paying by card online, or choosing pay-on-pickup.
  3. That shop's kitchen screen lights up with an alarm the instant the order lands.
  4. Staff tap once to Accept (and give a time), then Preparing, then Ready.
  5. The customer watches live status on their phone, so they know exactly when to collect.

6. Online payments — in simple terms

Customers pay by card in Australian dollars, with GST handled correctly so your books stay clean. Apple Pay and Google Pay are included. There's no setup or monthly fee on the card payments — you pay a small fee per order only when money comes in: 1.7% + 30c for Australian cards (3.5% + 30c international) — far below app commissions. You can also offer pay-on-pickup. Refunds and cancellations are controlled by managers only.

7. Built to grow

The system handles a busy Friday night without breaking a sweat, and adding a shop is straightforward — each location gets its own menu, prices, hours and order board, and orders route to the right kitchen. Later we can add loyalty, gift cards and full multi-shop/franchise reporting.

The best part — every new shop is commission-free too. You never start paying a slice per order, no matter how many shops you open.
